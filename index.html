About Us

Our laboratory is a civil materials testing facility dedicated to providing accurate and reliable testing services for construction materials. The laboratory is equipped with modern testing equipment and experienced technical staff to perform tests in accordance with relevant Indian Standards. We support engineers, contractors, government departments, and construction companies in maintaining quality control of materials used in infrastructure and building projects.

Mission

  • Support engineers and contractors
  • Improve laboratory equipment and expertise
  • Deliver timely reports

Laboratory Facility
